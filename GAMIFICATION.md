# 🎮 Gamification Mechanics

This document explains the complete reward and progression system that makes habit tracking fun and motivating!

## 🎯 Points System

### Earning Points

Points are the primary currency in the app. You earn them by completing habits:

- **Base Points**: Each habit awards points when completed (default: 10 points)
- **Custom Points**: You can set different point values when creating habits
- **Streak Bonuses**: Extra points for maintaining consecutive completion streaks

### Point Values Strategy

Recommended point values based on difficulty:
- **Easy habits** (5-10 points): Quick tasks like drinking water, making bed
- **Medium habits** (15-25 points): Activities requiring effort like exercise, reading
- **Hard habits** (30-50 points): Challenging goals like studying 2 hours, meditation

## 🔥 Streak System

### How Streaks Work

1. **Starting a Streak**
   - Complete a habit for the first time: Streak = 1
   - Complete it again the next day: Streak = 2
   - Continue daily to build your streak!

2. **Maintaining Streaks**
   - Must complete habit each calendar day
   - Streak increments only if completed yesterday or today
   - Missing a day resets streak to 0

3. **Longest Streak**
   - Your personal record is always tracked
   - Shown in Statistics tab
   - Never decreases, only updates when beaten

### Streak Bonus Points

The longer your streak, the more bonus points you earn:

| Streak Length | Bonus Points | Total (10pt habit) |
|---------------|--------------|--------------------|
| 1-2 days      | +0           | 10 points          |
| 3-6 days      | +1           | 11 points          |
| 7-13 days     | +2           | 12 points          |
| 14-29 days    | +3           | 13 points          |
| 30+ days      | +5           | 15 points          |

**Example**: A habit worth 20 base points with a 30-day streak gives 25 points per completion!

### Streak Tips

✅ **Best Practices**:
- Set realistic daily habits you can maintain
- Complete easier habits first to build consistency
- Check the app daily to maintain awareness
- Use rewards as motivation for milestone streaks

❌ **Avoid**:
- Setting too many habits initially
- Making habits too difficult to complete daily
- Forgetting to check in daily

## 🎆 Experience & Leveling

### XP Accumulation

- **Every completed habit awards XP equal to its points**
- XP never decreases or resets
- Tracks lifetime habit completions

### Level Progression

```
Level 1:   0 - 99 XP
Level 2:   100 - 199 XP
Level 3:   200 - 299 XP
Level 4:   300 - 399 XP
...
Level 10:  900 - 999 XP
Level 50:  4900 - 4999 XP
```

**Formula**: `Level = (Total XP ÷ 100) + 1`

### Level Benefits

- **Visual Progress**: See your level displayed prominently at the top
- **Milestone Celebration**: Each level represents 100 points worth of habits completed
- **Motivation**: Higher levels = more lifetime habit completions
- **No Cap**: Level infinitely as you maintain your habits!

### Progress Bar

The XP progress bar shows:
- Current XP within your level (0-100)
- How close you are to next level
- Updates in real-time as you complete habits

## 🎁 Reward System

### Creating Rewards

1. Navigate to Rewards tab
2. Tap the + button
3. Name your reward (e.g., "Movie Night", "Gaming Session")
4. Set the point cost
5. Save

### Reward Pricing Strategy

**Short-term rewards** (25-50 points):
- Small treats you can earn daily/weekly
- Examples: Coffee, favorite snack, 30 min break

**Medium-term rewards** (100-200 points):
- Weekly or bi-weekly goals
- Examples: Movie night, order takeout, buy a book

**Long-term rewards** (500+ points):
- Major milestones requiring weeks of consistency
- Examples: New game, day trip, expensive item

### Redeeming Rewards

1. Accumulate enough points
2. Navigate to Rewards tab
3. Tap "Redeem" on desired reward
4. Confirm redemption
5. **Points are deducted** from your total
6. Enjoy your reward in real life!

### Reward Tracking

- Each reward shows "Redeemed X times"
- Points are permanently deducted upon redemption
- Helps you see which rewards you use most
- No time limit on when to actually claim the reward

## 📊 Statistics Dashboard

Track your progress with key metrics:

### Total Habits
- Count of all active habits you're tracking
- Create more to earn points faster
- Balance quantity with quality

### Completion Rate
- Percentage: (Completed Habits) ÷ (Total Habits) × 100
- Updates as you complete habits each day
- Aim for 80-100% daily completion
- Lower rate? Maybe reduce habit count or difficulty

### Total Points Earned
- Lifetime points accumulated
- Includes all points from completed habits
- Includes all streak bonuses
- Does NOT decrease when spending on rewards
- **Different from Available Points** (shown at top)

### Longest Streak
- Your best consecutive completion streak
- Across all habits, shows your single longest
- Personal record that never decreases
- Challenge yourself to beat it!

## 🧮 Strategic Tips

### Optimal Progression Path

1. **Week 1**: Start with 3-5 easy habits
   - Build consistency
   - Learn the app
   - Establish routine

2. **Week 2-4**: Add medium difficulty habits
   - Maintain easy habit streaks
   - Earn growing bonus points
   - Start redeeming small rewards

3. **Month 2+**: Optimize your system
   - Focus on high-value habits
   - Leverage 30-day streak bonuses
   - Work toward big rewards

### Point Optimization

To maximize points earned:

1. **Prioritize Streaks**: A 30-day streak adds +50% bonus (15pts vs 10pts)
2. **High-Value Habits**: Do 25-point habits over 10-point ones
3. **Consistency > Quantity**: Better to complete 3 habits daily than 10 habits sporadically
4. **Don't Break Streaks**: Missing one day costs you the bonus AND resets to 0

### Reward Strategy

**Balanced Approach**:
- Keep some cheap rewards (25-50pts) for regular motivation
- Save points for occasional big rewards
- Don't spend all points immediately - having a reserve feels good
- Align rewards with your actual interests and desires

**Milestone Rewards**:
- 7-day streak: Small reward (50pts)
- 30-day streak: Medium reward (200pts)
- Level 5: Big reward (500pts)
- 100 total habits completed: Major reward (1000pts)

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Do I lose XP when I spend points on rewards?**
A: No! XP is permanent and only increases. Points are separate.

**Q: What happens to my streak if I miss a day?**
A: It resets to 0, but your longest streak record is preserved.

**Q: Can I edit a habit's point value?**
A: Yes! Long-press the habit and tap Edit. Changes apply to future completions.

**Q: Is there a maximum level?**
A: No! You can level up infinitely as you complete more habits.

**Q: What if I complete a habit twice in one day?**
A: Currently, each habit can only be completed once per day. This encourages daily consistency.

**Q: Do rewards disappear after redemption?**
A: No, they remain in your list so you can redeem them again! The counter increases.

**Q: Can I get points back if I accidentally redeem?**
A: Not currently - redemption is final. Future versions may add an undo feature.
